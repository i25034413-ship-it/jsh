The Pearl of Northern Anhui

Huaibei, also known as Xiangcheng, is a city steeped in over 4,000 years of history. Founded by Xiang Tu, the eleventh ancestor of the Shang Dynasty, it has grown from an ancient settlement into a modern city that beautifully preserves its cultural heritage.

Today, Huaibei stands as a shining example of green transformation, having evolved from a traditional coal mining city into an ecological garden city recognized nationally for its environmental achievements.
